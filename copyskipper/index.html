CopySkipper — Support

CopySkipper is a clean, one-way folder sync for macOS. Drag two folders, click Sync now, and optionally let Auto-run keep the target mirrored.

Quick answers

  • Cancel a running sync? Click Cancel sync.
  • What does “Mirror: delete extra items in the target folder” do? It removes items that exist only in the target. Use with care.
  • Auto-run not starting? Ensure both folders are selected and the timer is enabled.
